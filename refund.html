General Refund Notice

1. General Policy

Payments made on WorkDen.online are generally non-refundable once portal access and onboarding services have been activated. Upon successful payment confirmation, system credentials are generated and shared with the user.

Refunds are only considered in cases of duplicate payment, verified technical failure, or if portal access is not provided within a reasonable timeframe after successful payment.

2. Digital Service Nature

WorkDen provides digital access to task dashboards, training materials, and support protocols. Because these services are delivered instantly upon payment verification, they cannot be revoked once activated.

3. User Responsibility

Users are responsible for reviewing all service details and requirements before completing a transaction. By making a payment, you agree that you have understood the nature of our digital services.

4. Non-Refundable Scenarios

  • Change of mind after account activation.
  • Inactivity or failure to perform tasks after portal access.
  • Misunderstanding of platform guidelines.
  • Dissatisfaction unrelated to technical service failure.
  • Issues resulting from user-side device or internet connectivity.

5. Disputes

If you experience payment issues, please contact our support team at support@workden.online before initiating a dispute. Initiating false chargebacks may lead to permanent account termination.
